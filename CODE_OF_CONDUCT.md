# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors, maintainers, and community members of **Forensic Pro Suite** pledge to make participation in our project and community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

---

## Our Standards

### ✅ Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other community members
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and graciously accepting constructive feedback
- Accepting responsibility for mistakes, apologizing to those affected, and learning from the experience
- Focusing on what is best not just for us as individuals, but for the overall community
- Using inclusive and welcoming language in issues, pull requests, and discussions

### ❌ Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- The use of sexualized language or imagery, and sexual attention or advances of any kind
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information — such as a physical or email address — without their explicit permission
- Misusing project tools or infrastructure for unauthorized forensic activity or any illegal purpose
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

---

## Responsible Use Policy

Forensic Pro Suite is a digital investigation tool built for **educational and authorized forensic use only**. As a contributor or user, you agree to:

- Use the project **only on systems and data you own or have explicit written authorization to investigate**
- Never use any feature — including the Investigator CLI, hash pipeline, or geospatial tools — to conduct unauthorized surveillance, unauthorized data collection, or any illegal investigation
- Treat all forensic methodologies demonstrated in this project in accordance with applicable laws and ethical standards (NIST SP 800-86 and equivalent guidelines)

Violations of this policy may result in immediate removal from the community and may be reported to appropriate authorities.

## GSSoC Participation Guidelines

This project participates in **GirlScript Summer of Code (GSSoC)**. All contributors, mentors, and participants must additionally adhere to the following during the program period:

1. **No spamming** — Do not excessively copy-paste, flood channels with messages/memes/links, or send NSFW/obscene content in any project communication channel.
2. **No harassment** — Threats, cyberbullying, gender-based insults, or any form of internet attack toward fellow participants, mentors, or organizers will not be tolerated.
3. **Respect everyone** — Do not disrespect moderators, organizers, sponsors, admins, mentors, contributors, or industry professionals in any communication space.
4. **Use the right channels** — Post queries in the appropriate GitHub issues, discussions, or GSSoC Discord channels. Use project-specific channels for project-specific queries.
5. **No unsolicited DMs** — Do not directly message mentors, organizers, or admins without prior permission.
6. **Maintain a patient and positive attitude** — Open source thrives on encouragement and collaboration. Be supportive of fellow contributors throughout the program.
7. **Mentor commitment** — Mentors are expected to remain active and available to guide contributors for the full duration of the program.
8. **No unauthorized groups** — Do not create unofficial WhatsApp, Telegram, Slack, or other groups related to this project without organizer approval.

> Violation of GSSoC guidelines may result in disqualification from the program. Decisions are final and participation will not be reinstated.

For program-level concerns, report to the GSSoC organizing team via the official [GSSoC Discord](https://discord.gg/girlscript) or their official channels.

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior. They will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

Maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct, and will communicate reasons for moderation decisions when appropriate.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces — including the GitHub repository, issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other official channels — and also applies when an individual is officially representing the project in public spaces.

---

## Reporting

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers via:

- **GitHub Issues** (for non-sensitive concerns): [Open an issue](https://github.com/0504Siddhi/forensic-pro-suite/issues)
- **Direct contact**: Reach out privately to a maintainer via GitHub profile contact

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. The privacy and security of the reporter will be respected at all times.

---

## Enforcement Guidelines

Maintainers will follow these Community Impact Guidelines when determining consequences:

### 1. 📝 Correction
**Impact:** Minor, first-time inappropriate language or behavior.
**Consequence:** A private written warning with clarity on the violation. A public apology may be requested.

### 2. ⚠️ Warning
**Impact:** A single significant incident or a pattern of minor violations.
**Consequence:** A formal warning with consequences for continued behavior. No interaction with the people involved for a specified period.

### 3. 🚫 Temporary Ban
**Impact:** Serious violation of community standards, including sustained inappropriate behavior.
**Consequence:** A temporary ban from all community interaction and contribution.

### 4. ❌ Permanent Ban
**Impact:** Demonstrating a pattern of violations, harassment, or egregious conduct including misuse of forensic tools.
**Consequence:** Permanent removal from all project spaces.
